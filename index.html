About the musician

Hello, I am Nesterov

I am 27 years oldI've been enjoying the success that came to me 22 years after the release of my debut album, "Echoes of the River, for the past 5 years.

I live in a large country house on the banks of a beautiful river, which inspires me to create new music. Being alone with nature helps me to focus on my creativity.

My annual earnings are about $2.5 millionThis allows me not only to live comfortably, but also to support young talented musicians through my charity foundation.

Musical preferences

My music is a fusion of rock energy and classical depth. I draw inspiration from various genres to create a unique sound.

Rock

From classic 70s rock to modern alternative sounds. My main inspirations are Led Zeppelin, Muse and The Strokes.

Classic

Tchaikovsky, Rachmaninoff, and Chopin are composers whose works I often listen to before writing new compositions.

Fusion

I create music at the intersection of genres, combining electronic elements with live instruments and orchestral arrangements.
